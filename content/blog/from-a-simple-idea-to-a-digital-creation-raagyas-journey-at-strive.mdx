One weekend, Raagya had a simple idea — to make a card for her teacher. But instead of paper and crayons, she turned to her coding skills ✨ She imagined it, designed it, and coded a digital card entirely from scratch, transforming a small thought into something creative, unique, and truly special 💻

Raagya joined Strive in March 2025. When she first joined, she was initially hesitant to ask questions. Over time, she developed into an independent project creator who takes initiative and brings her own ideas to life.

Check out Raagya's card project in her [Strive portfolio](https://strivemath.com/portfolio/recHdoUOOXO8zyug5/project/recXFMrpkXp9nW4YF).
