But for years, our teachers struggled with messy workflows: writing questions, copy-pasting into Google Docs, manually formatting equations, exporting PDFs, and then sending them to students. It worked — but it was slow, frustrating, and prone to errors.

Now, we've solved that problem with our **new** [**PDF Generator in ChatGPT**](https://www.strivemath.com/pdf).

## From Prompt to PDF in Seconds

Our goal was simple: let teachers **generate ready-to-send PDFs with just one prompt**. No LaTeX knowledge. No manual formatting. No exporting chaos.

With the new system, teachers can:

- Type a prompt like: "Create a Grade 7 fractions worksheet with 10 questions and an answer key."
- Get a **professionally formatted PDF** instantly, complete with **math equations, graphs, and diagrams**.
- Download the PDF directly inside ChatGPT, ready to share with students.

## How It Works

The magic happens with:

- **MCP Server** — handles LaTeX-to-PDF compilation reliably.
- **Custom ChatGPT App** — integrates the PDF generation directly in your workflow.
- **Automatic formatting** — ensures every worksheet looks professional, clean, and ready to print.

Teachers no longer need to worry about broken PDFs, messy formatting, or wasted time. **One prompt → One professional PDF.**

- Coding Exercises
- Practice Tests
- Graphs, Tables, and Diagrams

And so much more. Everything is formatted automatically in LaTeX and compiled to PDF instantly.
